Why only two products?

Because we'd rather sell two things we can vouch for than two hundred we can't.

GimmiePets started the way most dog-gear stories do: a muddy retriever, a ruined back seat, and a pile of "universal" seat covers that all sagged, slipped, or soaked through. When we finally found a hammock design with a rigid bottom that actually held, we stocked it. The comb joined after it out-performed every de-shedding tool in the drawer during one long husky spring.

Everything in the store is something we use weekly with our own dogs. When we add a third product, it'll be because it earned the spot — not because a spreadsheet said the margin was good.

Questions? We answer within 24 hours.
