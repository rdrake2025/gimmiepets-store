Description

Most seat covers sag into the footwell, taking your dog with them. This hammock has a rigid, fold-flat bottom that bridges the gap and turns the whole back seat into one stable platform — your dog can stand, turn, and sleep without sliding around.

The quilted top layer is fully waterproof and scratch-resistant, so mud, hair, and the occasional car-sick moment stay off your seats. Installs in about two minutes with headrest straps and seat anchors; folds flat for the trunk when you need the seats back.
